    INTRODUCING

    Home Touch
    Care, Inc

    Home Touch Care, Inc is a non- medical home care agency in Massachusetts that promotes inclusivity and diversity and respects all human rights in delivering services. We offer our clients comfort, safety, and independence within the familiar surroundings of their own homes or anywhere they feel comfortable to help them recover or adapt to their condition more quickly. We provide our services to disabled, aging, and ailing people in group homes, assisted living facilities, nursing homes, or in their own homes. Our clients are entitled to receive high-quality, personalized care from care professionals at an agreed schedule. Hence, we strive to provide the highest possible level of care.
